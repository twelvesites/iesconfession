Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2025

At IES TEA, your privacy and anonymity are our highest priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website.

What Information We Collect

  • Anonymous Confessions: We do not require or collect any personal information when you submit confessions. All submissions are anonymous.
  • Device Information: We collect limited device data such as device type and browser details solely to identify and prevent misuse of the platform. We do not collect your IP address or any data beyond what is necessary for this purpose.

How We Use Your Information

  • To identify and prevent misuse or abuse of the platform.
  • To maintain the safety and integrity of IES TEA.

We do not collect or use your data to analyze or improve the website or services.

Data Sharing and Security

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties.
  • We use appropriate security measures to protect the data collected.

Cookies and Tracking

  • IES TEA may use cookies or similar technologies to provide site functionality.
  • You may control cookie settings via your browser, but disabling cookies may affect some site features.

Your Rights

  • Since no personal data is required or collected to submit confessions, you can remain fully anonymous.
  • For any questions or concerns regarding your data or this policy, DM us @iestea.app

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect any changes. Updates will be posted here with an updated effective date.
