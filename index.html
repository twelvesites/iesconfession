Welcome to IES Tea ☕

An AI-moderated space for IES students to vent, confess, and drop the hottest tea — safely. 💬🤖

What’s this about? 👀

This platform is built for IES students — a safe, anonymous corner to spill thoughts, rants, confessions, or even some juicy gossip 👀. Yes, there's drama, but there's zero room for hate, harassment, or bullying. Every post goes through AI moderation 🧠✨ to keep things respectful. It’s your space to be real — just don’t use it to harm others.
We’re here to vibe, not to violate. ✌️

📜 Code of Conduct

  • 💬 Your Voice Matters: This platform is for honest, anonymous expression — feel free to vent, confess, or share what's on your mind.
  • 🤖 AI Moderated: All confessions go through AI checks to catch anything harmful, offensive, or unsafe before it's posted.
  • ❌ No Bullying or Hate: Attacks, insults, threats, or targeting others will get removed.
  • 🔞 Keep It Clean-ish: We allow rants and real talk, but no NSFW, abusive, or explicit content.
  • 🔍 No Exposing: Don’t use this space to reveal secrets, spread rumors, or stir unnecessary drama that puts others at risk.
  • 🚨 Report It: If a confession targets you, makes you uncomfortable, or feels wrong — report it immediately. We’ll take action.
  • 🏫 Respect the School Space: This is still a student platform. Don’t post anything that could seriously harm someone’s well-being or reputation.
